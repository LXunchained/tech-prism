TechPrism integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API to allow users to publish video content directly to their own TikTok accounts from within the TechPrism platform. When you connect your TikTok account:

  • OAuth Authorization: You will be redirected to TikTok's official authorization page, where you explicitly grant TechPrism permission to act on your behalf. TechPrism never accesses your TikTok password.
  • Scopes Used:
    • video.publish — to publish videos you have prepared to your TikTok account
    • video.upload — to upload video files to TikTok's servers before publishing

  • Your Content, Your Account: TechPrism only publishes content that you have explicitly scheduled or approved. We do not post content without your authorization.
  • Token Storage: Your TikTok access token is stored securely and used solely to perform actions you've authorized. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok's app settings (TikTok Settings → Manage Apps).
